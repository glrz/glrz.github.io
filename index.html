About

I am a Cybersecurity Analyst at PUB, Singapore’s National Water Agency, holding a Master of Science in Cybersecurity from Nanyang Technological University and a Bachelor of Computer Science (Cybersecurity) with distinction from the University of Wollongong. During my undergraduate studies, I earned top academic honours, including placement on the Dean’s Merit List (Top 5%) and the IRAS Silver Award for securing second place in the Cybersecurity programme.

With expertise spanning digital forensics, malware analysis, open-source intelligence (OSINT), and cyber threat intelligence, I have competed in over 50 Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges and regularly publish technical write-ups to both deepen my knowledge and contribute to the cybersecurity community. Driven by a passion for solving real-world security challenges, I leverage automation, advanced threat intelligence platforms, and established cybersecurity frameworks to strengthen organizational resilience and optimize operational efficiency.
